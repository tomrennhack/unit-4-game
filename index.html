Crystals Collector

You will be given a Random Number at the start of the game.

There are four Crystals below. By clicking on a Crystal you will add a specific amount of Points to your Total Score.

You win the game by matching your Total Score to the Random Number. You lose the game if you allow your Total Score to become greater than the Random Number.

The value of each Crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

When the game restarts after you Win or Lose, the value of each Crystal will be changed randomly.
